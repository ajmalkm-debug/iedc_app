# Ideation Hackathon Thrissur — Rules & Guidelines

**Date:** April 9, 2026
**Venue:** IEDC Lab, Block C, Government Medical College, Thrissur
**Organized by:** IEDC LaunchPad, Govt. Medical College Thrissur

---

## Eligibility

- Open to all health science students in Kerala (MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, B.Pharm, Nursing, Allied Health Sciences)
- Individual or team participation (max 4 members per team)
- At least one team member must be currently enrolled in a recognized institution
- Faculty and mentors may advise but not participate as team members

## Submission Format

Each submission must include:

1. **Problem Statement** — Clear description of the clinical problem being addressed
2. **Proposed Solution** — Innovation concept with basic design/approach
3. **Feasibility Assessment** — Resources needed, estimated cost, timeline
4. **Impact Statement** — Expected patient benefit and scale of impact
5. **Presentation** — 5-minute pitch + 3-minute Q&A to the jury

Submissions must be made through the IEDC LaunchPad platform before the deadline.

## Judging Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Clinical Relevance** | 30% | How significant is the clinical problem? Is there genuine unmet need? |
| **Innovation** | 25% | How novel is the approach? Does it offer a meaningful improvement over existing solutions? |
| **Feasibility** | 25% | Can this be built with available resources? Is the timeline realistic? Is the cost target achievable? |
| **Impact** | 20% | How many patients benefit? What is the potential for scaling across Kerala/India? |

**Total:** 100 points per jury member. Final score is the average across all jury members.

## What Happens to Top 10 Ideas

The top 10 ideas selected by the jury will receive:

- **Incubation support** through the IEDC LaunchPad programme
- **Dedicated mentor** assigned from faculty or industry partners
- **Access to prototyping facilities** in the IEDC Lab
- **Fast-track entry** to the MedTech Innovation Exhibition (April 11, 2026)
- **Ongoing tracking** through the LaunchPad platform with milestone-based progression

Ideas will enter the LaunchPad pipeline at the "Submitted" stage and progress through: Submitted → Incubating → Prototyping → Validating → Complete.

## Rules

1. All ideas must be original work of the participating team
2. Pre-existing projects may be submitted but must be disclosed
3. Use of AI tools for ideation is permitted but must be acknowledged
4. All intellectual property remains with the student innovators
5. IEDC reserves the right to showcase submissions for institutional purposes
6. Decisions of the jury are final
7. Code of conduct: Respectful behavior, no plagiarism, no disruption
