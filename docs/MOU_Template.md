## 1. PURPOSE

This Memorandum of Understanding establishes a framework for collaboration between IEDC, Government Medical College Thrissur, and the Partner for the purpose of advancing clinical innovation, medical device development, and healthcare technology solutions.

## 2. SCOPE OF COLLABORATION

The parties agree to collaborate in the following areas:

- Joint development of medical innovation prototypes
- Sharing of technical expertise and mentorship resources
- Access to laboratory facilities and testing infrastructure
- Co-organization of hackathons, workshops, and innovation events
- Student internship and industry exposure programmes
- Clinical validation and pilot testing of innovations

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 3.1 IEDC shall:
- Provide access to clinical insights, problem statements, and healthcare domain expertise
- Facilitate student teams for innovation projects
- Provide workspace and basic prototyping facilities
- Ensure compliance with institutional ethics and research guidelines
- Assign a designated coordinator for the partnership

### 3.2 Partner shall:
- Provide technical mentorship and industry expertise
- Offer access to specialized equipment, software, or manufacturing facilities as agreed
- Support commercialization pathways for validated innovations
- Assign a designated liaison for the partnership

## 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

- Pre-existing intellectual property of each party shall remain with the respective party
- Jointly developed intellectual property shall be co-owned, with specific terms to be agreed upon for each project through a separate IP agreement
- Student innovators shall retain moral rights to their innovations
- Any patent filing shall be discussed and agreed upon by both parties before submission
- Revenue sharing from commercialized innovations shall be determined on a case-by-case basis

## 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information shared during the collaboration. A separate Non-Disclosure Agreement may be executed for specific projects involving sensitive information.

## 6. DURATION

This MOU shall be effective from the date of signing and shall remain valid for a period of **two (2) years**, unless terminated earlier by either party with 30 days written notice.

## 7. FINANCIAL ARRANGEMENTS

This MOU does not create any financial obligations between the parties. Any financial commitments shall be subject to separate agreements.

## 8. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this MOU by providing 30 days written notice to the other party. Ongoing projects at the time of termination shall be completed or wound down in an orderly manner.

## 9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes arising from this MOU shall be resolved amicably through mutual consultation. If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, with the venue in Thrissur, Kerala.

## 10. GOVERNING LAW

This MOU shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India, with jurisdiction in the courts of Thrissur, Kerala.
